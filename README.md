# Data Skills Bootcamp 
A collection of recent projects demonstrating skills in Excel, Tableau, Power BI, SQL, Azure, and Python. 

his document provides a detailed overview of the competencies and knowledge acquired through a Data Technician training program. The key areas covered include:

*   **Governance and Compliance Essentials**: Emphasises adherence to UK data laws, such as GDPR and the Data Protection Act, to ensure ethical data handling, organisational integrity, and risk mitigation from breaches.
*   **Excel and Data Manipulation Skills**: Highlights proficiency in Excel functions, data structuring and cleaning, and advanced formulas such as VLOOKUP to transform raw data into actionable information.
*   **Pivot Tables for Data Analysis**: Describes pivot tables as essential tools for summarising large datasets, identifying trends, and enabling dynamic exploration for business insights.
*   **Data Visualisation and Interpretation**: Covers the creation of clear charts and dashboards to communicate complex data effectively, as well as the importance of accurate interpretation for strategic decisions.
*   **Strategic Use of Data Insights**: Focuses on integrating technical skills with critical thinking to foster data literacy, optimise operations, and align data strategies with organisational goals.
*   **Workbook Practical Skills Development**: Demonstrates the application of Tableau and Power BI for data cleaning, dashboard creation, trend analysis, and interpretation of real-world health data, reflecting growth in technical, analytical, and professional competencies.
*   **Database and SQL Fundamentals**: Details an understanding of relational database concepts, key constraints, SQL JOIN types, and practical database design for retail, including security and maintenance best practices.
*   **Cloud Computing and Legal Frameworks**: Explains cloud service models (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS), deployment types, and relevant legal regulations for data handling, illustrating the benefits and alternatives to cloud adoption.
